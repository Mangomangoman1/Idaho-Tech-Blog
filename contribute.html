Contribute tips and evidence

Send an Idaho technology tip, source, or field note.

The Idaho Stack collects field notes, interviews, corrections, company tips, documents, and source leads from people close to Idaho technology: founders, operators, technicians, educators, students, public-sector workers, and small businesses.

Email a tip or field noteSee interview format

Good submissions

  • Founder or operator notes
  • AI experiments inside Idaho businesses
  • Repair, hardware, field-tech, or infrastructure lessons
  • Agtech, broadband, energy, or workforce observations
  • Student, educator, researcher, or technician perspectives

Formats

  • 150–300 word field note
  • 5-question Q&A interview
  • Contributed article or commentary
  • Anonymous background tip for research

Editorial promise

Contributions may be lightly edited for clarity, length, and factual precision. We do not fabricate quotes. Contributors can review their own quotes or field notes before publication. Promotional submissions may be labeled or declined. Corrections are welcome.

Prompt you can answer

  1. Who are you and where in Idaho are you based?
  2. What technology, workflow, company, or system are you close to?
  3. What changed recently?
  4. What do people outside Idaho misunderstand about this?
  5. What should Idaho builders pay attention to next?
